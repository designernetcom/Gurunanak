Why Modern Manufacturers are Replacing the Furnace

Traditional furnace stress relieving requires massive energy consumption, long turnaround times, and high transport costs. Formula 62 Vibratory Stress Relieving (VSR) eliminates these bottlenecks entirely.

Evaluation Parameter Traditional Furnace Stress Relieving Our Formula 62 VSR Service
Processing Time Furnace takes 12 to 24 hours to complete. VSR takes 30 to 45 minutes to stabilize
Component Size Limitations Strictly limited by physical furnace dimensions Unlimited size, length, and weight capacities
Surface Finish Impact Causes heavy oxidation, scaling, and soot layers. Leaves metal surface 100% clean and pristine
Transportation Needs Requires expensive low-bed trailers for heavy transport. Completely mobile; performed directly on your factory floor
Dimensional Distortion Risk High risk of gravity sagging or thermal warping forces Zero thermal distortion risk; preserves geometric tolerances
Energy Footprint Extremely high fuel, gas, or electricity consumption grid draw Eco-friendly, highly efficient low-power operation

Financial Advantages: Where You Save Money

Direct Financial Impact on
Your Production Costs

Switching to Formula 62 VSR significantly improves your bottom line across multiple operational areas

  • Eliminate Freight Expenses: Stop paying for heavy transport cranes and specialized trailers to move massive components to a remote furnace.
  • Zero Post-Treatment Cleaning Costs: Because VSR doesn't create furnace scale, you save 100% of the costs associated with post-heat shot blasting, pickling, or acid cleaning.
  • Drastically Reduced Machining Allowances: Since there is no thermal warping, your engineers can design components with smaller machining allowances, saving raw material weight and cutting tool wear.
  • No Scrap Risks: Delicate or thin-walled complex castings face a high risk of cracking in a furnace under sudden thermal shock. VSR eliminates this structural risk entirely.
Turnaround Time: From Days to Minutes

Accelerate Your Production Timeline

In heavy engineering, lead time is everything. Waiting for a commercial furnace queue, transit schedules, heating cycles, and cooling periods can delay a project by 3 to 7 days.

With our At-Site mobile VSR services, your component is structurally stabilized in under an hour. This allows your team to move immediately from welding to final precision machining, cutting your total manufacturing cycle time drastically.
