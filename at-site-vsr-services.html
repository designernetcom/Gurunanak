Core Service Overview & Logistics Elimination

Eliminating Material Distortion & Transport Logistics Entirely

Our specialized At-Site PAN India Mobile Vibratory Stress Relieving (VSR) service resolves these structural vulnerabilities and operational bottlenecks completely. Managed out of our central office in Pune, we transport our specialized patented Formula 62 systems straight to your facility via our regional hubs in Ghaziabad, Rourkela, and Bangalore. We introduce targeted mechanical energy right where your assets stand. This induces micro-plastic deformation at the crystalline boundaries permanently neutralizing internal stresses in under an hour to guarantee absolute geometric stability while completely eliminating transportation freight costs and shortening project timelines.

Heavy-duty manufacturing processes like multi-pass welding, flame cutting, and rough machining lock severe residual tensile stresses deep into the atomic lattice of metals. Left untreated, these internal forces release progressively over time, causing finished components to warp out of alignment during final grinding or fracture prematurely under real-world operational loads.

Transporting oversized or heavy engineering structures to a static heat-treatment furnace is a logistical nightmare. It requires expensive low-bed trailers, special road permits, police escorts, and introduces a high risk of handling damage or structural twisting during transit.
