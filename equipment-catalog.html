The Science Behind Formula 62 Resonant Technology

How We Neutralize Residual Stresses at the Molecular Level

Metals change at a structural level when they are welded, gas-cut, or heavy-machined. High local heat locks severe tensile stresses into the atomic lattice. Over time, these stresses release naturally, causing the metal to warp, twist, or crack.


Our Formula 62 system uses high-amplitude, low-frequency resonant vibrations to target these high-stress zones. The machine identifies the exact resonant frequency of your structure and applies controlled energy. This induces micro-plastic deformation right at the crystalline boundaries, safely shifting and neutralizing internal stresses in under an hour.
