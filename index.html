Company Introduction — Headquartered in Pune, Deployed Nationally

Setting the Standard in Industrial Dimensional Stabilization

Guru Nanak Stress Relievers Pvt. Ltd. is India's premier, 100% specialized provider of At-Site Mobile Vibratory Stress Relieving (VSR) services. Headquartered centrally at our Main Office in Pune, we manage all national logistics, technical consultations, and client communications from a unified core desk. We operate completely as a field-deployed engineering force—we do not run a static workshop and we do not do generic furnace heat treatment. Our entire focus is dedicated to delivering non-thermal structural stabilization directly to your facility.

By transporting our specialized equipment straight to your manufacturing line or assembly yard through our network of strategically placed branch nodes, we permanently lock in micron-level geometric accuracy. Our mobile operations eliminate the high transport freights, low-bed trailer logistics, and transit warping risks of traditional thermal stress relieving.
