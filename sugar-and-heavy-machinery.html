High-precision structural stabilization for heavy rotating assets, milling components, and process equipment using Formula 62 technology. Managed centrally via our Main Office in Pune.

Request Sugar Mill VSR Quote

Preventing Equipment Breakdown During Peak Seasons

Sugar factories and heavy processing plants operate under extreme, continuous mechanical loads. Components like mill rollers, cutters, and heavy-duty gear systems endure massive torque and cyclic stresses. When these parts are repaired by welding, deep internal stresses are also locked into the metal.

If these stresses are not relieved, the components will warp during final machining or fracture under load, causing catastrophic downtime during peak crushing seasons. Our Formula 62 VSR service distributes these residual stresses quickly and safely without heat, ensuring maximum mechanical reliability and extending equipment life.
