Neutralizing Weld Stresses in Critical Process Equipment

Vessels operate under severe cyclic loading, extreme pressures, and highly corrosive chemical environments. The heavy longitudinal and circumferential welding required to join shell courses and dish ends leaves high levels of residual tensile stress.

Left untreated, these hidden stresses trigger Stress Corrosion Cracking (SCC) along weld zones, leading to catastrophic field failures. Our specialized Formula 62 VSR process redistributes these peak tensile stresses at a sub-microscopic level. This permanently protects your vessels from environmental cracking while preserving the original material ductility and thickness.
