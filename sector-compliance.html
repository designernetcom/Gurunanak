Core Industries We Support

High-Precision Stress Relief Across Major Industrial Verticals

From high-speed rotating equipment to static structural frameworks, our Formula 62 services ensure maximum operational reliability across major sectors.

Sugar & Process Industries

  • Scope: Supporting sugar factories, refineries, and chemical process lines.
  • Applications: Heavy crushing mill housings, cane cutters, roller shafts, vacuum pans, and evaporator body shells.
  • Value: Prevents seasonal equipment breakdowns, preserves weld integrity, and eliminates logistics delays during critical production windows.

Oil, Gas, & Petrochemicals

  • Scope: Servicing vessel fabricators and refinery equipment suppliers.
  • Applications: Reactor shells, storage tank sections, tube sheets, and heavy-wall piping spools.
  • Value: Eliminates high tensile weld stresses to effectively prevent Stress Corrosion Cracking (SCC) in harsh chemical environments.

Power Generation & Energy Infrastructure

  • Scope: Supporting thermal, hydro, wind, and nuclear power plant equipment builders.
  • Applications: Wind turbine base flanges, stator frames, turbine casings, and heavy steam headers.
  • Value: Guarantees dimensional stability under heavy cyclic vibrations and high operational loads.

Machine Tool & Precision Manufacturing

  • Scope: Partnering with CNC machine builders, foundries, and high-precision tool rooms.
  • Applications: Long machine beds, slide ways, robotic welding fixtures, and large injection mould bases.
  • Value: Locks in structural geometry down to the micron, completely replacing the need for months of old-fashioned weather seasoning.

Infrastructure & Heavy Engineering

  • Scope: Serving major structural fabricators and heavy machinery builders.
  • Applications: Massive overhead crane girders, earthmover excavator booms, heavy press frames, and long structural columns.
  • Value: Prevents twisting and warping of massive structures without size or weight handling limitations.
